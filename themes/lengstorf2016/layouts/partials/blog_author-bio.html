About the Author: Jason Lengstorf

I’m living Post-Hustle.

After years spent in the “Hustle & Grind” culture — the always-on, anything-for-success slog in pursuit of More™ — I finally asked myself the question I should have asked a long time ago: “Why do I work?”

I work to give myself a better life. Work shouldn’t be standing between me and an enjoyable life — and that’s what Post-Hustle is all about.
